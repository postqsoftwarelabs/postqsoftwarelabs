We are building solutions to help organizations transition to post-quantum cryptography through a structured, repeatable approach.

Why This Matters

Advances in quantum computing will reshape digital security. Cryptographic systems trusted today may not remain secure in the future. Preparing early enables a controlled and low-risk transition.

Our Focus

Cryptography Visibility

Understanding where and how cryptography is used across systems.

Crypto Agility

Designing systems that can adapt as cryptographic standards evolve.

Dependency Awareness

Evaluating third-party, hardware, and supply chain dependencies.

Operational Readiness

Supporting phased rollout and hybrid cryptographic strategies.

PQC Migration

Enabling phased transition from current cryptographic systems to quantum-safe standards.

Governance & Strategy

Establishing policies, ownership, and long-term transition planning.

What We’re Building

  • • Discovery and mapping of cryptographic usage across applications and systems
  • • Risk-informed prioritization for PQC transition
  • • Insights to guide phased migration strategies
  • • Tooling to analyze implementation patterns and identify cryptographic dependencies within code and runtime environments
  • • Support for gradual adoption of quantum-safe standards

About the Name

PostQ represents the transition to post-quantum security—where systems are designed to remain secure in the presence of quantum computing.

Software Labs reflects continuous exploration, experimentation, and building practical solutions for real-world challenges.
